# ArrowKnockbackEnchantment

## Purpose
Handles the "Punch" enchantment for bows, which increases the knockback force applied to targets hit by arrows.

## Key Classes
- **ArrowKnockbackEnchantment**: Inherits from `Enchantment`.

## Modding Notes
Increases the `knockback` value of the spawned `Arrow` entity.

## Important Dependencies
- `Enchantment.h`
