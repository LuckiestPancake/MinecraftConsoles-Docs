# AnimalChest

## Purpose
A specialized container used for animal-related inventories, such as horse equipment or llama carpets.

## Key Classes
- **AnimalChest**: Inherits from `SimpleContainer`.

## Important Functions
- `AnimalChest()`: Initializes the container with a specific size and title (`IDS_CONTAINER_ANIMAL`).

## Modding Notes
Basically a renamed `SimpleContainer` for better organizational clarity in the code.

## Important Dependencies
- `SimpleContainer.h`
