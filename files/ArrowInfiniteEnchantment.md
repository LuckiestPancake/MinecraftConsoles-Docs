# ArrowInfiniteEnchantment

## Purpose
Handles the "Infinity" enchantment for bows, allowing players to fire arrows without consuming them from their inventory.

## Key Classes
- **ArrowInfiniteEnchantment**: Inherits from `Enchantment`.

## Modding Notes
Checked by the `BowItem` class before consuming an arrow.

## Important Dependencies
- `Enchantment.h`
